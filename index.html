HEY BARBIE

Welcome to our dazzling datastory. We’re hoping you’ll enjoy the ride. Here in Data-land, we have selected the wonderful ‘movie dataset’. This will help us answer one question: could we have predicted Barbie’s blockbuster success over Oppenheimer’s this summer? We’ll look at this through different lenses. First, how have movie genres performed overall, and how could it relate to Barbenheimer’s success through that analysis? Second, is there a general sentiment behind movie plots and how does it relate to their revenue? Third, do movie fanatics express a certain affinity with movie characters' personalities, and does that contribute to movie popularity? Don't leave us on a cliffhanger! Stick around with us for an explosive conclusion, which may or may not inspire you for your next holiday cozy night.
